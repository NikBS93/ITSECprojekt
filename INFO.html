Welcome to Cybersecurity 101: Protecting Your Digital World

1. UPDATE YOUR SOFTWARE

Keep your operating system, apps, and antivirus up-to-date.

Regular updates patch security holes, keeping hackers out.

on automatic updates for hassle-free protection.

2. STRONG PASSWORDS

Use unique, complex passwords for each account.

Avoid common phrases and consider a password manager for easy management.

3. TWO-FACTOR AUTHENTICATION (2FA):

Add extra security layers to your accounts.

Pair passwords with a code sent to your phone for added protection.

4. WATCH FOR PHISING:

Be cautious of suspicious emails or calls asking for personal info.

Verify sources before clicking links or downloading attachments.

5. SECURE YOUR HOME NETWORK

Protect your Wi-Fi network with a strong, unique password and enable encryption.

Consider hiding your network's SSID and regularly update your router.

6. PRACTICE SAFE BROWSING:

Exercise caution when browsing the web.

Stick to reputable websites for sensitive activities, ensure URLs start with "https://".

7. REGUARLY BACK UP DATA:

Protect your valuable files by regularly backing them up to external drives or cloud storage.
